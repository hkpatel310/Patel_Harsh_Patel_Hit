popular Cocktails


Introducing the Sunshine Spritzer, a dazzling cocktail that embodies the essence of a sunny day. This invigorating blend combines the refreshing flavors of orange with a hint of sparkle, leaving you feeling revitalized and uplifted. To create this delightful concoction, simply mix 2 ounces of orange vodka, 4 ounces of sparkling water, and a splash of freshly squeezed orange juice. Add a dash of honey or agave syrup for a touch of sweetness. Shake or stir the ingredients with ice, then strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a twist of orange peel or a sprig of fresh mint. With its vibrant citrus notes and effervescent charm, the Sunshine Spritzer is the perfect companion for outdoor gatherings, brunches, or any occasion that calls for a burst of zestful flavor. Raise your glass, soak up the sun, and let this captivating cocktail transport you to a state of pure bliss. Cheers to the radiance of Quatro!

Introducing the Orange Blossom Martini, a cocktail that captures the delicate beauty and exquisite flavor of oranges. This sophisticated libation is a harmonious blend of citrus and floral notes, perfect for those seeking a refined and elegant drink. To craft this enchanting martini, combine 2 ounces of orange-infused vodka, 1 ounce of freshly squeezed orange juice, ½ ounce of elderflower liqueur, and a dash of orange bitters in a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake vigorously to achieve a frosty chill and strain the mixture into a chilled martini glass. For an extra touch of elegance, garnish with a twist of orange peel or a floating edible flower. Sip slowly and allow the Orange Blossom Martini to transport you to a world of pure sophistication and indulgence. With every sip, you'll be captivated by the graceful allure of Quatro's orange-infused creations. Raise your glass and toast to the art of mixology and the beauty of a well-crafted cocktail. Cheers!
